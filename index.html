Welcome to Xecured

Public store of material for Indus University. Upload your own solution or find one!

This website works on principle :
By the students, For the students.
Which means you are uploading the material and you are getting the material. However, all the content will be verified by me and then only be published.
If someone does not upload material, I will not be able to publish on this site. As simple as that!
To send your material, send it via email : me@xecured.cf
Alongwith the material, please specify the semester, branch and subject.

Books

Find textbooks and reference books provided by the faculties and some top-level books from the internet.

Assignments

Find the assignments from respective branches as per your semester.

Solutions

Find assignment solutions, or you can upload your own!
